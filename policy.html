Child Protection Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment: Solar Community is dedicated to creating a safe and secure environment for all users, especially minors. We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that could harm children.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing a safe digital space where young people can express themselves, learn, and connect with others while being protected from harmful content and predatory behavior.

Age Requirements

Minimum Age

Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account. Users under 18 are considered minors and are subject to additional protections under this policy.

Parental Consent

For users between 13-17 years old, we encourage parental involvement and may require parental consent for certain features or activities.

Prohibited Content Related to Minors

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Sexual or suggestive content involving minors
  • Content that exploits or endangers children
  • Bullying, harassment, or intimidation of minors
  • Sharing personal information of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Dangerous challenges or activities that could harm minors

Safety Measures

Content Moderation

We employ both automated systems and human moderators to:

  • Review and remove inappropriate content
  • Monitor user interactions for suspicious behavior
  • Respond quickly to reports of harmful content
  • Enforce community guidelines consistently

Privacy Protection

We implement enhanced privacy protections for minor users:

  • Limited data collection from minors
  • Restricted contact features for users under 16
  • Enhanced default privacy settings
  • Parental control options

Reporting System

How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior that violates our child protection policy:

  • Use the report button on any content or profile
  • Email our safety team directly
  • Contact our support team through the help center
  • For emergencies, contact local authorities first

Our Response

We take all reports seriously and will:

  • Investigate reports promptly
  • Take appropriate action against violators
  • Provide support to affected users
  • Cooperate with law enforcement when necessary

Education and Resources

We provide educational resources to help users stay safe online:

  • Digital safety guides for different age groups
  • Resources for parents and guardians
  • Regular safety awareness campaigns
  • Community education programs

Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Account suspension or termination
  • Restrictions on platform features
  • Reporting to law enforcement
  • Legal action when appropriate

Emergency Situations

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services immediately. Our platform should not be used as a substitute for emergency services.
