Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 2025

Our Vision: Solar Community is a place where creativity flourishes, friendships are formed, and everyone feels welcome to express themselves authentically while respecting others.

Our Core Values

These guidelines are built on our core values:

  • Respect for all community members
  • Encouragement of creativity and self-expression
  • Inclusivity and diversity
  • Safety and well-being of all users
  • Authenticity and genuine connections

Be Respectful

✓ Do:

  • Treat others with kindness and empathy
  • Respect different opinions and perspectives
  • Provide constructive feedback when appropriate
  • Celebrate others' achievements and creativity
  • Help newcomers feel welcome

✗ Don't:

  • Engage in harassment, bullying, or intimidation
  • Use hate speech or discriminatory language
  • Make personal attacks or insults
  • Share personal information without consent
  • Engage in trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

Share Responsibly

✓ Do:

  • Share your original creative work
  • Credit sources and collaborators appropriately
  • Use appropriate tags and content warnings
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights
  • Share content that contributes positively to the community

✗ Don't:

  • Post inappropriate or offensive content
  • Spam or post repetitive content
  • Share stolen or plagiarized content
  • Spread misinformation or false claims
  • Post excessive commercial or promotional content

Content Standards

Allowed Content

We welcome diverse creative content including:

  • VOCALOID music, covers, and original songs
  • Cosplay photos, tutorials, and discussions
  • Gaming content, reviews, and discussions
  • Digital art, traditional art, and creative projects
  • Educational content and tutorials
  • Community discussions and social interactions

Restricted Content

The following content is not allowed:

  • Adult or sexually explicit content
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Content promoting illegal activities
  • Content promoting self-harm or suicide
  • Content promoting dangerous activities

Community Participation

💡 Tips for Great Community Participation:

  • Engage meaningfully in discussions
  • Ask questions and seek help when needed
  • Share your knowledge and expertise
  • Support fellow creators and their work
  • Participate in community events and challenges

Reporting Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the report feature on posts or profiles
  • Contact our moderation team
  • Provide specific details about the violation
  • Use blocking features to protect yourself

Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning and educational guidance
  • Content removal
  • Temporary restrictions on features
  • Account suspension
  • Permanent ban for severe violations

Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error:

  • Submit an appeal through our support system
  • Provide context and explanation
  • Wait for our team to review your case
  • Accept the final decision respectfully

Staying Updated

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. We will notify the community of significant changes. It's your responsibility to stay informed about current guidelines.
